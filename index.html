Scandinavian-style beetroot, apple and walnut soup

Serves 4,
Prep time: 15 mins, Cook time: 10-15 Min

Ingredient's

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 peeled and chopped eating apple
  • 1 tsp caraway seeds
  • 1 chopped red onion
  • 75g Toasted walnuts
  • 500ml Vegetable stock
  • 300g Chopped cooked beetroot
  • 200g chopped pickled cooked beetroot
  • 8 x 1 slices of vegetarian goat's cheese
  • Chopped toasted walnut and a few fresh dill sprigs, to serve

Directions:

Step One: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large casserole (with a lid), add the apple, caraway seeds and chopped red onion. Cook over a medium heat with the lid on, stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes until caramelised.

Step Two: Add the toasted walnuts and cook for another 3 minutes, then pour in the vegetable stock. Add the chopped cooked beetroot and the chopped pickled cooked beetroot. simmer for 2 minutes, then pour into a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.

Step Three: Return to the pan to keep warm and season to taste. Heat the grill to its highest setting. Put the goat's cheese slices on a baking tray lined with non-stick baking paper, then grill for 4-5 minutes until golden and bubbling.

Step Four: Divide the soup amon 4 bowls and top each with 2 slices of goat's cheese, a sprinkle of chopped toasted walnuts and a few fresh dill sprigs.
